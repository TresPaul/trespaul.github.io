About

Students for a Democratic Society South Africa formed in 2019 at Stellenbosch University. A group of students started a Political Philosophy reading group, but soon found the literature ununderstandable and stale, and their frustrations with local, national, and international politics too immediate. That group of students formed a prefigurative organisation with the goal of spreading political knowledge and literacy, and encouraging skepticism about those elements of society we take for granted.

Our Constitution can be downloaded on the Resources page.

Values

As a society, we hold the following values as central to our organisation and vision:

  • Humanism
  • Solidarity & Mutual Aid
  • Prefiguration
  • Horizontalism
  • Education

Goals

The founding members of SDS came together with the following mutual goals, and formed the society around them:

  • Conscientisation: education about the things we take for granted about society;
  • Education: bridging the gap between academia and the general (student) population;
  • Organisation: bringing like minds together, so we can work together on our mutual goals;
  • Growth: to challenge our echo-chambers — self-crit;
  • Outreach: to encourage campus and wider societal interaction.
